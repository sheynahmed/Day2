Alappuzha

The Venice of the East

Infinite Horizons

Where the waves meet ancient history

A Labyrinth of Azure Waters

Alappuzha is the hub of Kerala's backwaters. Crisscrossed by a network of canals, it is famous for its majestic Kettuvallams (houseboats) that float lazily over the Vembanad Lake.

Whether it is the adrenaline of the Nehru Trophy Boat Race or the serene sunsets at the Alappuzha beach with its 150-year-old pier, this district remains the most romanticized destination in God's Own Country.
