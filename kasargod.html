Kasaragod

Northern Horizons

A tapestry of diverse cultures and languages

The Gateway of Northern Kerala

Kasaragod is the northernmost district of Kerala, world-renowned for its coastal forts and its status as a melting pot where Malayalam, Tulu, Kannada, and many other languages coexist.

It is home to the majestic Bekal Fort, the largest and best-preserved fort in Kerala, which stands tall against the Arabian Sea. From the peaceful Valiyaparamba backwaters to the sacred Madhur Temple, Kasaragod is a land of serene beauty.
