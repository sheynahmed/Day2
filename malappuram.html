Malappuram

The Hill-top City of Golden History

Sacred Shores

Tranquility along the banks of Bharathapuzha

Culture, Sports, and Nature

Malappuram, which literally translates to "Hill Top", is a land of ancient wisdom and modern sporting passion. It is famous as the cradle of football in Kerala and the center of Ayurvedic healing at Kottakkal.

The district is bordered by the Western Ghats on the east and the Arabian Sea on the west. From the world's oldest teak plantation in Nilambur to the historic temple town of Thirunavaya, Malappuram offers a diverse cultural landscape.
