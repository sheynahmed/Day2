Wayanad

The Green Paradise of the Western Ghats

Untouched Nature

Where the mist meets the mountains

A Highland Escape

Wayanad is a pristine bio-diversity hotspot located high on the majestic Western Ghats. Its name is derived from 'Vayal Nadu', which translates to the 'Land of Paddy Fields'.

This district is a treasure trove of Neolithic history, seen in the Edakkal Caves, and offers some of the best trekking trails in Kerala. From spice plantations to tribal colonies and gushing waterfalls, Wayanad is the ultimate destination for nature lovers.

Wayanad’s Natural Wonders

Banasura Sagar Dam

The largest earth dam in India, surrounded by mesmerizing islands.

Soochipara Falls

A three-tiered waterfall cascading into a natural pool, perfect for a dip.

Pookode Lake

A scenic freshwater lake shaped like India’s map, nestled among hills.
