Idukki

The Powerhouse of God's Own Country

Mist-Clad Peaks

Wander through the emerald tea carpets of Munnar

Where the Clouds Touch the Earth

Idukki is Kerala's most nature-rich district, characterized by high-altitude mountains, dense tropical forests, and deep valleys. It is home to Anamudi, the highest peak in South India.

From the engineering marvel of the Idukki Arch Dam (the first of its kind in Asia) to the wildlife sanctuaries of Thekkady, Idukki is an adventurer’s paradise and a sanctuary for those seeking the cool mountain breeze.
