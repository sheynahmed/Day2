Thiruvananthapuram

The Evergreen City of India

Golden Sands

Explore the pristine shores of Kovalam

A Blend of Tradition & Progress

Thiruvananthapuram, also known as Trivandrum, is built on seven hills and serves as the gateway to the tropical paradise of Kerala. Named after the sacred serpent Anantha, the city is a unique mix of colonial architecture, ancient temples, and India's first IT park, Technopark.

From the world's richest temple, Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, to the tranquil backwaters and high-tech corridors, it offers an experience like no other.
