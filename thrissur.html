The Land of Festivals

Thrissur is world-renowned for the Thrissur Pooram, the most spectacular temple festival in India. It is a city built around a massive teak forest and the grand Vadakkunnathan Temple.

As the heart of Kerala's heritage, Thrissur is home to the Kerala Kalamandalam—a prestigious center for performing arts like Kathakali—and boasts the majestic Athirappilly Waterfalls, the largest fall in the state.
