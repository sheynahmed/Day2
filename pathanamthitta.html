The Spiritual Soul of Kerala

Pathanamthitta is often called the 'Pilgrim Capital of Kerala' as it serves as the gateway to the world-renowned Sabarimala Temple. Over 50% of this district is covered by dense forests, making it a sanctuary for biodiversity.

Beyond its religious significance, it is home to the stunning eco-tourism destination of Gavi and the legendary elephant training centers of Konni. It is a place where the rhythmic chants of temples meet the silent whisper of the wild.
