Kottayam

The Land of Letters, Latex, and Lakes

Akshara Nagari

The Gateway to Highlands and Backwaters

The Cultural Heart of Central Kerala

Kottayam is a land uniquely defined by three 'L's: Letters (as the first district to achieve 100% literacy), Latex (for its massive rubber plantations), and Lakes (fringed by the Vembanad Lake).

From the world-class bird sanctuary in Kumarakom to the ancient churches and temples that showcase religious harmony, Kottayam offers a sophisticated blend of nature and heritage.
