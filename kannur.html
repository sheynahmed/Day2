Kannur

The Land of Looms and Lore

Ancient Strongholds

Where the waves crash against history

Echoes of Folklore & Shorelines

Kannur is a coastal gem with a storied past. Known for its traditional handloom industry and the powerful Theyyam ritual dance, it is a district where spirituality and craftsmanship go hand in hand.

From the massive sea-facing St. Angelo Fort built by the Portuguese to the unique experience of driving along the sands of Muzhappilangad Beach, Kannur offers a rustic, authentic Kerala experience.
