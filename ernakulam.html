Ernakulam

The Queen of the Arabian Sea

Fort Kochi

Where History Whispers in Every Corner

The Commercial Capital

Ernakulam is the commercial and industrial heart of Kerala. It is home to Kochi (Cochin), a historic port city that has welcomed traders from around the world for centuries.

The district is a fascinating study in contrasts: from the 16th-century Jewish Synagogue and Portuguese architecture in Fort Kochi to the ultra-modern skyline of Marine Drive and the bustling Kochi Metro.
